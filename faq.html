Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about buying and selling steel.

How do I become a verified supplier?

To become a verified supplier, simply register on our "Sellers" page. Our team will review your company details, certifications, and product portfolio. Once approved, you can start receiving enquiries from global buyers.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQs vary depending on the product type and the supplier. Generally, for international shipments, the MOQ is one container load (approx. 25 tons). However, we can facilitate smaller orders for specific high-value items.

Do you handle logistics and shipping?

Yes, World of Steel offers end-to-end logistics support. We handle freight booking, customs documentation, and inland transportation to ensure your steel arrives on time and in perfect condition.

What payment methods are accepted?

We support secure international payment methods including Letter of Credit (LC), Telegraphic Transfer (TT), and Document Against Payment (DP), ensuring safety for both buyers and sellers.

Can I request a sample before placing a bulk order?

Yes, samples can be arranged for most products. Please contact our sales team with your specific requirements, and we will coordinate with the manufacturer to send you a sample.
